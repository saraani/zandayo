Complete with surreal elegance, the Halong Bay is a picturesque destination and tops the list of best places to visit in Vietnam. It is known for its turquoise blue water, sinkhole lakes, marine cliffs, limestone caves, grottoes, and islets. This amazing area is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and features a wide spectrum of biodiversity. Its floating villages also offer a magnificent sight to behold. The crystal clear water is perfect for indulging in various water adventures like kayaking, cruising, diving, etc. Adventure lovers also love to indulge in cave exploration and discover natural wonders that lie underneath..
